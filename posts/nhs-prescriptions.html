Sensing the real world through NHS prescriptions data

September 2021 · News

Researchers interested in geography of health need principled ways to create proxies for health outcomes from official datasources. Over the past 2 years at Bell Labs, I've been working on the NHS Prescriptions Parser — allowing practitioners to mine openly available NHS prescriptions data to make geo-spatial estimates about health outcomes across England.
