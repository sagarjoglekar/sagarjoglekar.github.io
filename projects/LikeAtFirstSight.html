Like at First Site: User engagment study of Micro-videos

Micro-videos are a new and popular form of social media, which is used by the so called "mellenial" generation to express themselves. Our aim in this project is to understand user engagement with these micro-videos and verify the computationally acquired insights that we have. For this reason we would appreciate if you could take this survey and contribute to the user study
